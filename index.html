Core Engineering Services

Providing high-precision hardware and software solutions optimized for the demands of modern wireless ecosystems.

Automated Validation Systems

Design and deployment of custom Hardware-in-the-Loop (HIL) validation systems. We integrate programmable RF attenuators, Python automation, and modern CI/CD pipelines to validate complex product interfaces and software states autonomously.

  • GitHub Actions & Jenkins CI Integration
  • Dockerized Microservice Test Runners
  • Python-Based Automation Suites

Embedded SoC & Wireless Characterization

Deep characterization and validation of embedded wireless silicon. We specialize in WLAN/Bluetooth coexistence (coex) profiles, OTA radiated measurements, and link budget/path loss modeling.

  • WLAN / BT Coexistence (Coex) Tuning
  • OTA Radiated Performance characterization
  • Link Budget & Path Loss modeling

Pre-Compliance & Test Readiness

Prepare your product for formal regulatory and industry testing. We perform critical pre-scans and test validation focusing on regional parameters (FCC/ETSI), supported frequency domains, dynamic frequency selection (DFS), and protocol readiness to reduce certification risks.

  • FCC / ETSI Parameter Audits
  • DFS & Frequency Domain Verification
  • WFA & Regulatory Test Readiness Prep

[ ENGINEERING PHILOSOPHY ]

Full-Stack Validation & Real-Time Observability

At Black Alder Technology LLC, we believe that validation is critical to software and product health. We deliver robust full-embedded-system validation services led by seasoned industry experts and driven by custom-built automation suites. Our approach prioritizes real-time dashboarding and deep system observability, allowing teams to track performance metrics and device health continuously.

By pairing exhaustive Layer 2+ protocol conformance checks with real-time telemetry, we capture transient anomalies before they impact the field. While we specialize in L2 and upper-layer interoperability today, we are actively expanding our testbeds to integrate Layer 1 Vector Signal Generators (VSG) and Vector Signal Analyzers (VSA) for high-fidelity physical-layer auditing.
